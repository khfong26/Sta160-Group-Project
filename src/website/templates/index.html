Job Market Analytics Dashboard

Explore real-time insights into salaries, top skills, and market trends across the U.S. tech landscape.

How is the current job market looking for Statistics majors? Using various APIs, we expand upon indeed.com's job-searching abilities!

Using the jobspy library, we webscraped thousands of postings across several categories of jobs on indeed, and gathered data on their salary ranges, skill sets, and locations via plotly.

More specialized tools like SpaCy and PhraseMatcher were used to get an overview of the skills in demand for this job market.

The interactive component of this website, the recommendation model under "Resume Match", will take your resume and show you a sample of suitable job postings, after converting your resume and the job descriptions into numerical vectors via and analyzing cosine similarity.
